FINANCIAL HEALTH CHECKER

The financial health checker is another tool lemonade used to help users to determine the financial situation. By asking a series of questions, we score each question and at the user is given a score out a 100. The questions are separated in to categories eg mortgage, will, the icons have 3 colour states red orange and green. This way the user can see where they fall short.

The category cards give different advice depending on the score for that section and for here they are directed to other web tools the help the user improve there score and lemonade can make more leads.
